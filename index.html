Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering


The Department of Industrial Management, Faculty of Science, University of Kelaniya proudly announces the launch of the new B.Sc. in Software Engineering degree program for students from the physical science stream. This is the first ever Software Engineering degree in Sri Lanka to be offered for a batch of fifty students directly through UGC selection catering to a long felt need for software industry in Sri Lanka.se.
This degree program will help you to gain core software engineering knowledge and skills during the first two years of the program. The students can then specialize in the application areas of Net-centric applications, Mobile computing, Business intelligent systems, Health informatics, Digital games and animations, and Business engineering.
